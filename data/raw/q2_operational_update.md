# Q2 Operational Update

Quarterly operations improved in engineering and sales, while support and finance continued to lag target performance.

Key highlights:

- Sales conversion improved after pricing simplification.
- Engineering delivery predictability improved because backlog grooming was tightened.
- Support margins remained weak because of rising ticket volume and contractor usage.
- Finance closed the quarter on time but reported manual reconciliation overhead.

Leadership should monitor support utilization, contractor dependence, and margin leakage over the next quarter.
