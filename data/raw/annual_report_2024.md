# Annual Report 2024

The company closed 2024 with stronger enterprise demand and a healthier product mix.

Leadership noted that revenue expanded steadily over the year, driven by the enterprise and digital media segments. The board also noted margin pressure in the support organization because of rising onboarding costs and slower automation rollout.

Strategic priorities for the next planning cycle are:

- improve enterprise retention,
- reduce support cost to serve,
- accelerate automation in customer operations,
- improve forecasting discipline across business units.

The largest risks highlighted by leadership were slower hiring in data engineering, operational bottlenecks in support, and uneven department execution.
